Synopsis & Inspiration

Tavipop (The Siam Renaissance) is a classic Thai story originally written by Tomayandee about a girl who travels back in time through an antique mirror. The story has been adapted over the years to fit film, musical, and drama formats. This year's TCN is an adaptation of the same Tavipop story, modified to showcase more aspects of Thai culture through additional travel to Thailand's different regions.

TCN's Tavipop is a story about Manee, a Thai girl raised abroad, who returns to Thailand to rediscover her heritage. She purchases an antique mirror that draws her through to Siam in the 1800s, where she meets Khun Luang, the Minister of Foreign Affairs at the time. She learns how to behave in the traditional Thai standards for a woman while simultaneously using her skills in foreign language to help Khun Luang with his work. However, back in the modern time, Manee's best friends, Ploy and Win, go on an adventure of their own in their search of the missing Manee.
