Thai Culture Night 2013 Director

Irada Wattanavitukul
"Irada is an international student from Thailand, she received her degree in spring 2012 with Summa Cum Laude and is on her way to a PhD program in Clinical Child Psychology. Currently she works and is under training as a pediatrics and clinical child psychology researcher and an ABA Therapist at UC Irvine Medical Center.

She was inspired by her favorite childhood novel written by Tomayantee that includes many aspects of Thai Culture and History. She wrote the script for this year's Thai Culture Night and oversaw all the committees that helped make this culture night possible. She has a very strong Thai cultural background and was also trained in Thai traditional classical dance since the age of 6, she is also the founder of our Thai traditional dance crew."
