  • Kanyarat Konsue

    Manee (Manie)

    Raised abroad, Manee has lost touch of what it means to be a proper Thai lady. She is bright and charismatic, with a hint of playfulness that often gets reprimanded by Khun Luang. She struggles between traditional and modern values because she finds use in both.

  • Maytin Mahasiri

    Khun Luang

    He is a traditional, strong, gentleman figure with a sense of responsibility to his family and his country. He is curious about things that are new, but also reluctant to deviate from the accepted norms.



  • Alyssa Kumnoonsate

    Prayong

    A naive family friend of Khun Luang's who was betrothed to him since birth. She is a typical Thai proper lady who knows little of the outside world and relies on others. Very innocent and pure.



  • Tanisa Niranatkul

    Ploy

    She is all about the trendy and modern. A city-girl at heart, Ploy has little interest in anything "old school". However, as she explores the outer regions of Thailand, she starts to find that "old school" might be making a comeback.

  • Tyler Nutsathitya

    Win

    A typical Thai guy who has to put up with Ploy's extreme girliness. His head stays level while her's floats in the clouds. The perfect combination for someone who will soon move out of the friendzone.


  • Sahavatchara Tirabaedya

    Raywat

    An arrogant, rich, city-boy who thinks money is the solution to all problems.





  • Siamrath Boonsakul

    Sir Visarn

    Khun Luang's superior who orchestrated Manee's ability to travel through time. The "wise old man" of the story.



  • Charlene Konkankit

    Khun Ying Sae

    (Khun Luang's Mother)

    Firm lady of the house who prides herself on running a respectable household with a sense of humor.

  • Jom Amtawong

    Muan

    Main servant who is practically a family member, resulting in forgetting her place from time to time. Comes from a common, low-class background and therefore has great interest in all things new.

  • Alice Park

    Manee's Mother

    Loves Manee very much but is always busy working. Manee and her mother have a great relationship and are able to talk to each other about anything.
