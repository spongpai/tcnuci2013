Thai Culture Night 2012: In Another Lifetime


Synopsis: The show itself featured a skit along with performances by a modern dance group and traditional Thai songs. The pre-show featured the band Fireworks.

*** All proceeds went to the recovery efforts from the flooding occurred in Thailand ***

Thai Culture Night 2011: Thai Mahanakorn - City of Dangerous Passion


Synopsis: Our play, "Thai Mahanakorn - city of dangerous passion" was a spectacle of music, dance, and drama. With an international cast, we were proud to take you on a journey to experience Thai culture set in the heart of Thailand itself. The journey was filled with danger, suspense, and love overcoming all obstacles.

*** All profit went towards aiding underprivileged children in Thailand ***
