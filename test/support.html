All donations and support are very welcome

Thai Culture Night is Thai Club's annual stage production, in which we invite all members of our community to attend. We encourage everyone to enjoy and to learn about the Thai Culture. The production will be held in late April where the students and members of Thai Club will be able to put on a performance incorporated music, dance, drama, and the visual arts.

We would greatly appreciate all the help and support we can get from the community. Donations, services, and materials for the production are all welcome. Please contact us for more information.
